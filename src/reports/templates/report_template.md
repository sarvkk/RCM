## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Research Questions

1. **Circuit Localization**: Is refusal behavior localized to specific neural network components?
2. **Direction Extraction**: Can we identify a "refusal direction" in activation space?
3. **Causal Validation**: Can we manipulate refusal through targeted interventions?
